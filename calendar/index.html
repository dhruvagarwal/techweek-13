About Us
- □ ✖
About IEEE-DTU

IEEE student branch of Delhi Technological University also known as IEEE DTU student branch (formerly IEEE DCE student branch) is the oldest student branch in Delhi Section of Region 10 (Asia Pacific). With a history spanning over 30 years IEEE DTU has come a long way. It is the head of HUB 3 of IEEE Delhi section under the GINI (Global Integrated Network of IEEE) project and mentor of IEEE branches of elite colleges like BITS Pilani. IEEE DTU is also the largest student branch in Delhi section since it has the largest number of student members.


About TechWeek

IEEE DTU every year organises a series of workshops under the name TECHWEEK as a first step in building the technical excellence among the freshers. Workshops organized under TECHWEEK help the students acquaint with their respective field of interest. It intends to help students stimulate their interests in the cutting edge technologies of today.

TECHWEEK also brings non technical workshops within its ambit. Eminent speakers have come and equipped students with mantras on personality development and success.
